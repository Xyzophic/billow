How to use Billow
  1. Lie flat on your back somewhere comfortable.
  2. Place your phone on your belly, screen up, just below your ribs.
  3. Breathe normally. The first reading takes about 30 seconds.
  4. Then slow your breath — long, easy exhales — and watch the number drop.
Billow auto-detects which way your breathing moves the phone. The screen stays awake during a session. All sensing happens on your phone — nothing is recorded or sent anywhere.
Billow is a relaxation and breathing-awareness tool, not a medical device. It does not diagnose, treat, or monitor any health condition. Readings are estimates and depend on phone placement. If you feel dizzy or short of breath, stop and breathe normally. Consult a clinician for any concerns about your breathing.
